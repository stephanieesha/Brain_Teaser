ABOUT GAME


ESHA'S Memory Game is a fun game created by Stephanie Ayuba Esha, as her final project
for She Code Africa Cohort2 mentoring program



Players can test their attention to details and improve their memory with more practice



Players can also choose various levels based on their preference

About Levels

Easy:

Cards shuffle for 6 seconds

Player takes note of positions of itentical cards for 4 seconds before they flip


Normal:

Cards shuffle for 3 seconds

Player takes note of positions of itentical cards for 2 seconds before they flip


Hard:

Cards shuffle for 1.5 seconds

Player takes note of positions of itentical cards for 1 second before they flip
