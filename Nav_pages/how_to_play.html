HOW TO PLAY

1. Enter your name on the homepage

2. Select a level and you would be directed to the game page

3. On the game page, take note of the positions of the identical cards as they shuffle

4. When the cards flip, try to match as many identical cards as you can

5. To play again, press the Refresh button


Click the 'Back to Home' button below ot the 'Home' menu to return to the homepage
